PhD Candidate in Economics — University College London

Welcome! I am a PhD candidate in Economics at University College London and a PhD Scholar at the Institute for Fiscal Studies. My research interests lie at the intersection of Empirical Industrial Organisation and Structural Econometrics. I am working on developing new tools to study economies of scope and market competition for multiproduct firms.
